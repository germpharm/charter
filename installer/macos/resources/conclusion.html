Your governance identity has been created. Here's what to do next:

If you use VS Code Search for "Charter Governance" in the Extensions panel and install it. You'll see a governance indicator in your status bar.
Check your status Open Terminal and type: charter status
View your dashboard Open Terminal and type: charter serve Then open the link in your browser.
Your governance data is stored locally at ~/.charter/
Configuration lives in charter.yaml in your project directory.
Updates: charter update
